Privacy Policy

Offline Music

This page explains how the Offline Music app handles user data, local storage, device permissions, and privacy-related practices.

Effective Date June 23, 2026
App Type Offline music player

1. Information We Collect

Offline Music does not require account creation and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

The app may access the following data stored on your device:

  • Local audio files
  • Playlist data created by the user
  • Playback preferences and app settings
  • Equalizer and sleep timer settings

This data is used only to provide app functionality.

2. How We Use Information

The app uses local device data only for the following purposes:

  • Scanning and displaying music files
  • Playing audio stored on your device
  • Creating and managing playlists
  • Saving playback position and preferences
  • Supporting shuffle, repeat, equalizer, and sleep timer features

3. Data Storage

All user data managed by Offline Music is stored locally on your device. This may include playlists, favorite tracks, playback settings, equalizer preferences, and library scan results.

Offline Music does not upload your music library or user settings to any external server.

4. Data Sharing

Offline Music does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

The app does not use advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or tracking services.

5. Permissions

Offline Music may request the following permissions:

  • Audio / Storage access: to scan and play local music files
  • Notifications: to show playback controls and status
  • Foreground service: to support active playback when allowed by the system
  • Boot completed: to restore scheduled playback-related features if enabled by the user

These permissions are used only for app functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

Offline Music does not rely on third-party services for analytics, advertising, or user tracking.

If the app later integrates a third-party service, this policy will be updated accordingly.

7. Children’s Privacy

Offline Music is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not collect personal data, no child-specific user data is stored on our servers.

8. Security

Because the app stores data locally on the device, the security of that data depends on the security of the device itself.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Privacy Policy for Offline Music Effective Date: June 23, 2026 Offline Music is an offline music player application designed to play audio files stored on your device. Information We Collect - Offline Music does not require you to create an account. - Offline Music does not collect, sell, or share your personal information. - The app may access local audio files on your device only to scan, display, and play music. - The app may store local settings such as playback preferences, sleep timer, equalizer settings, and playlists on your device. Permissions Used - Storage / Media Audio: used to scan and play music files stored on your device. - Notifications: used to show playback controls and playback status if enabled. - Foreground service playback: used to continue music playback when permitted by the system. - Boot completed: used only for scheduled playback-related features such as alarms or timers if enabled. How Data Is Stored - All playlists, settings, and playback preferences are stored locally on your device. - Offline Music does not upload your music library or settings to any server. Third-Party Services - Offline Music does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services. Children’s Privacy - Offline Music is not intended to collect personal data from children. - If you believe data has been provided in error, please contact us.
